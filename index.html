How To Play

  1. You start by dividing all cards equally. You can use any number of cards from 20, to all cards. Lets say you are using 30 cards. You give 15 to each player.
  2. Whoever had more coffee today starts the game. They can either put a situation card in the middle, or pick a location card and a trait, and say the number. Be careful, pick the highest number you have. Then, the other person will find the place that has a higher score of that trait. If they have, they will take that card to themselves. Whoever has the higher number keeps both cards. After the exchange completes, the person whose turn it is can play a situation or place card. Alce Battle consists of

1) Location Cards

These cards have traits of the places like flavor, romance, speed. Before playing, your role is to discuss the values of each trait of each place with your significant other, and mark it on the cards with a Sharpie. It might be a battle, but the field rules have to be decided in fair terms.

2) Situation Cards

These cards are powerful, and when played it it effective until another situation card gets thrown on it. These cards have the power to multiply, lower, or sum up points, depending on colors placed next to the traits on cartes de lugares. There are different type of situation cards:
